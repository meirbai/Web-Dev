Travel to Hawaii

Why Hawaii?

Hawaii is one of the most popular places in the world.

Advantages and disadvantages of visiting Hawaii:

  • Exotic and beautiful place
  • Access to the beach
  • Huge amount of attractions and entertainments for tourists
  • A lot of new hobbies and activities
  • New types of food and drinks
  • Expensive to book
  • Usually crowded
  • Expensive food/entertainment

Overall, it is a good choice for good vacation!
